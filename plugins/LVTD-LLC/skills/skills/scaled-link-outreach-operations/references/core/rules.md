# Scaled Link Outreach Operations Rules

Use these rules when operating bulk or semi-bulk link outreach.

## Core Rules

### 1. Scale Only Proven Fit

- Confirm the asset, offer, and prospect segment fit before increasing volume.
- Do not scale a campaign with weak replies or poor placement quality.

### 2. Segment Before Templating

- Segment by audience, page type, motivation, and ask.
- Use separate templates for materially different contexts.
- Reserve high-value targets for manual outreach.

### 3. Use Meaningful Personalization

- Personalize around page context, audience need, broken resource, prior mention, or beat.
- Avoid fake personal lines that do not affect the request.

### 4. Pilot And Diagnose

- Send 5-10 percent of the list or a small representative batch.
- Review bounces, replies, complaints, and qualitative feedback.
- Revise offer, segment, subject, or evidence before expanding.

### 5. Maintain Compliance And Suppression

- Include accurate identity and contact details.
- Honor opt-outs and maintain suppression lists.
- Check current laws, platform policies, and client constraints for the target geography and send type.

## Red Flags

- One template targets multiple unrelated site types.
- The personalization variable can be removed without changing the pitch.
- The list source is unclear or stale.
- No pilot threshold is defined.
- Opt-outs, bounces, and complaints are not tracked.
