# Relationship Led Link Building Knowledge

Core concepts for building links through useful participation and collaboration.

## Key Concepts

### Useful Action Before Asking

Useful action before the ask changes the relationship context. Examples include citing a publisher, sharing work, inviting expertise, introducing people, or giving a platform to another person's ideas.

### Content Promotion Network

A network of experts, publishers, partners, customers, and community members can help content travel when they are genuinely included or helped.

### Participation Asset

Expert surveys, group interviews, writing assignments, webinars, roundups, and partner resources create natural reasons for participants to share and cite.

### Relationship State

Track whether someone is unknown, aware, warmed, contributor, partner, advocate, or inactive. Do not treat all prospects as cold leads.

## Helpful Tools

- CRM or spreadsheet for relationship state and history.
- Social listening and feed readers for useful engagement opportunities.
- Content calendar for features, expert requests, and promotion dates.
- Outreach platform for one-to-one messages and follow-up.
- Analytics, social metrics, and backlink tools for relationship outcomes.
